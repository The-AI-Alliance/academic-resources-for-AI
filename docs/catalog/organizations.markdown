# Non-profit Organizations Focused on AI in Academia

Organizations that advocate for safe and effective use of AI in academic, as well as other settings. 

{: .note }
> **Help Wanted:** Add your resources to this catalog! See [contributing]({{site.baseurl}}/contributing) for details.

## Alliance for Data Science and AI

[website](https://alliance4datascience.ai/){:target="_blank"}

{: .attention}
> **Quote:** ADSA is a professional association for data science and AI in higher ed. We support academic institutions to launch, grow, and evolve their data science and AI initiatives through strategic guidance, community expertise, and resources grounded in real institutional practice.

## The AI Alliance

[website](https://www.aialliance.org){:target="aia"}

(The AI Alliance maintains this website.)

Among the many AI Alliance projects are two initiatives for academic AI.

### Academic Education and Research Forum

This forum holds monthly discussions about how AI is used in member institutions. Please join us! See the [Contributing]({{site.baseurl}}/contributing/#join-the-forum) page for information about joining.

### Guide to Essential Competencies for AI

This guide was prepared by Alliance members and published in 2024. It is not limited to academic contexts, but discusses the required competencies for everyone.

[web page](https://thealliance.ai/core-projects/guide-to-essential-competencies-for-ai){:target="_blank"}, [PDF](https://thealliance.ai/hubfs/Essentials%20Guide%20to%20Applying%20AI/Guide%20to%20Essential%20Competencies%20for%20AI.pdf?hsLang=en){:target="_blank"}
